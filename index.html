Decentralization

The liquidity pool is permanently locked, and the ownership of the smart contract is transferred to the black hole address on the date of issue. The contract on heco smartchain is 0xFeD613CdD4182b73A032fe1f03Bd7872465010F8

Shrink rules

Each transfer transaction on the chain will be taxed by 10%, of which 3% will be put into the black hole in real time and destroyed automatically.

Smart contract dividend

3% of each transaction in the chain is allocated to all holders. Each transaction on the chain will add 4% tax deduction to the mdex fund pool to provide liquidity.

Community autonomy

The development of C-token community is completely driven by the self-built C-token community, which is the core of C-token.

There are 4 simple functions about C token

One

1. Reflection & LP acquisition& combustion. 10% fee is levied on each transaction, and divided into three parts.

Two

2. The 3% fee is redistributed to all existing holders, enabling a new model of mining.

Three

3. The 4% fee is automatically repurchased and injected into the bottom pool in order to strengthen the depth of its LP pool, which has been permanently locked and cannot be withdrawn;

Four

4. The 3% fee goes into the black hole. In a nutshell, the more frequently the C token exchanges, the more valuable it is.
